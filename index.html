About Me

4 Years. 3 Countries. Always Shipping.

Full-Stack Developer & MSc student in the UK with 4+ years in Angular, Java, and Spring Boot — seeking a part-time role.

I'm based in London, United Kingdom, currently completing my MSc while working in frontend engineering. Over the last 4 years I've built and shipped production-grade applications for insurance portals and fintech platforms serving clients across Malaysia, the Philippines, and Nepal.

My journey started at S.B Solutions in Nepal, where I led frontend development for a Loan Management System used by banks and micro-finance institutions. I then moved to SenangNvs in Malaysia, specialising in Angular-powered insurance portals with complex RESTful and GraphQL integrations.

My philosophy: understand the business domain deeply before writing a single line of code, build for maintainability, and treat every API contract as seriously as a product spec.
